SALES MACHINE
Priyanshu only enters at step 4. Ayush qualifies. Asset pre-sells. Call closes. Vedant closes the backend.
01
TOP OF FUNNEL — TRAFFIC
Organic Instagram stories + reels, long-form YouTube, paid Meta ads. All channels → same CTA: DM "OPNSCALE". ManyChat auto-captures, fires first qualifier. Ayush takes it from there.
Channels: IG / YT / Meta
CTA: DM OPNSCALE
Owner: Priyanshu
02
AYUSH — SETTER QUALIFICATION
Ayush manually qualifies every inbound in DMs. Qualification: revenue stage, pain clarity, timeline, budget signal. Decision: send presale asset, or book call directly based on readiness level. Ayush does NOT close.
Owner: Ayush
Tools: IG DMs + ManyChat
KPI: Call-book rate
03
PRESALE ASSET
VSL/Loom sent before Priyanshu enters. Covers: the problem (invisible high-status founders), the mechanism (Identity Excavation), proof (Harshit case study + results), the three tiers, Priyanshu's story, Calendly CTA. The call should be a formality by the time they get here.
Format: VSL / Loom
Length: 10–15 min
Goal: Pre-kill objections
04
CLOSING CALL — PRIYANSHU
30–45 min. Lead has seen the presale asset, been qualified. Priyanshu: diagnose their specific situation, assign the right tier, anchor value, close. Vedant handles contract + payment within 1hr of verbal close.
Owner: Priyanshu
Tools: Calendly + Zoom
Post-call: Vedant
